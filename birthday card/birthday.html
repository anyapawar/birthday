Happy Birthday Mina!


Dear Mina,

Happy Birthday to an exceptional special girl! Mina. You are 20 years old now. You have grown into an incredible, strong, and independent woman. I am proud to call you my friend. Cheers to you and all your achievements. I hope you have a wonderous day filled with good laughs, good food and good times. To celebrate this moment, here is a special poem:

Every one of your birthdays
is a gift to the rest of us
who have had the pleasure
of having you in our lives
for another year.
Every one of your birthdays
brings joyful memories
of your bright smile
and your love of life.
Every one of your birthdays
reminds us of your sensitivity
and service to others,
and other delights
too numerous to name
that we are gifted with
just by knowing you.
Happy Birthday
to one in a million.
May you, and we
enjoy many more.

By Joanna Fuchs

Have a joyful birthday. From yours truly,

Anya
